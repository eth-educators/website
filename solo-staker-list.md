EthStaker has taken over maintenance of [Rated's solo staker list](https://github.com/rated-network/solo-stakers). EthStaker's first update is planned for July 1st, 2025 using [Rated's methodology](). After this point we expect to update the list quarterly.

The current [solo staker list](https://github.com/eth-educators/solo-stakers) is up to **validator index 1,500,000**. If you are a solo staker not included (check below), feel free to [file a petition](https://forms.gle/YFKFVgq2xdEHS2s26).
